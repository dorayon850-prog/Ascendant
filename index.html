📖 HOW TO USE ASCENDANT
▸ YOUR DAILY ROUTINE
Open the app → choose a preset tab → complete your missions throughout the day. The timer resets the protocol automatically at your set time each day. That's the full loop — the system handles the rest.
▸ PRESET TABS
🏋 Fitness — heavy physical training at the core, sharpened with mindset work.
💻 Focus — deep work and mental output, supported by movement.
⚖ Balance — an even split of body and mind.
🧘 Wellness — sleep, water, breathwork and recovery.
💼 Entrepreneur — output, strategy, learning and visibility.
⚙ Custom — build your own mission set. Name missions clearly — the voice system uses the names to find them.
Tap ✏ on any preset mission to rename or substitute it. Your edits persist across sessions.
▸ LOCKING THE PROTOCOL
Once every mission is complete, the LOCK PROTOCOL button becomes available. Tapping it seals the day early — the timer no longer applies and your perfect day is recorded. You cannot lock with any mission unfinished. The system enforces this.
▸ XP AND LEVELLING
Every completed mission earns XP and raises a stat. Completing all missions in a single day triggers a perfect day bonus — extra XP awarded the moment the last mission is ticked. A separate streak bonus is added the following day when the timer resets and your consecutive days are confirmed.
▸ STREAK RULES
Complete every mission in a day to build your streak. The streak is confirmed when the timer resets — not when you finish. Miss one day and the streak survives. Miss two or more consecutive days and it resets to zero. Milestones at 7, 14, 30, and 60 days unlock XP multipliers and trigger a system announcement.
▸ PENALTIES
When the timer resets with unfinished missions, the system applies automatic penalties — XP and stat deductions based on what was skipped. Fitness missions missed cost strength and agility. Focus missions missed cost intelligence. The penalty set shifts with your active preset. There is no way around them — finish your missions or face the cost.
▸ WEEKLY BOSS
A new challenge appears every Monday, scaled to your level. Log your progress with the + LOG button throughout the week. Clear it before Monday for a major XP reward. Tap ✏ to customise the challenge to match your goals.
▸ SKILL TREE
Passive abilities unlock automatically as you level up — XP boosts, double stat gains, reduced penalties, increased streak rewards. You don't activate them. Reaching the required level activates them for you.
▸ VOICE COMMANDS
Tap the mic and speak naturally. Say "complete [mission name]" to tick a mission. Say "missions" to hear what's left. Say "status" for your level, XP and streak. Say "ascend" for a system greeting. Custom missions are recognised by name — name them clearly.
▸ MODES
⚔ ASCENDANT — dark blue, military tone, physically demanding quests.
🌑 ECLIPSE — crimson gold, sovereign tone, mixed physical and mindset rites.
Switch anytime. Your level, XP and stats carry across both modes.
▸ THE TIMER
The countdown shows time remaining in your current day. Tap it to set your personal reset hour. When it reaches zero the system resets automatically — penalties apply for anything unfinished, streaks are confirmed for anything complete. The progress bar drains through the day. Red means time is running short.
